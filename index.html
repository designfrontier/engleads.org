Our Mission

EngLeads is dedicated to fostering a community of engineering leaders in Salt Lake City who are committed to personal and professional growth. We believe that strong leadership is the foundation of successful software engineering teams.

Our mission is to provide a platform for engineering managers to connect, share experiences, and learn from one another. Through regular meetups and knowledge-sharing initiatives, we aim to elevate the standard of engineering leadership in our community.

We focus on practical leadership skills, technical management challenges, and creating supportive networks that help engineering leaders thrive in their roles and advance their careers.
